Here at 1,100 meters, life follows the rhythm of the mountain. 

From the scent of freshly carved wood in the workshop to the patient fermentation of sourdough bread, every day is a practice of slow living. In our "Hidden Garden," we document the simple joy of coexistence—nurturing the food forest, observing local wildlife, and building a sustainable home among the Alps.
