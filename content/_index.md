---
title: "Il Giardino Nascosto"
---

This is a garden nestled in **Alpe Pianezzo**, 1,100 meters above sea level.

Here, our lives are interwoven among all living things, existing as an integral part of the ecological whole. We nurture a variety of projects, from the cultivation of food forests to insect and wildlife conservation, and the practice of green building.

Our world is a hidden garden, blossoming with endless surprises.

---

這是一座位於 **Alpe Pianezzo** 海拔 1,100 公尺的花園。

在這裡，我們將生活交織在萬物之間，作為整體生態環境的一員。我們進行各種計畫，包含培育食物森林、昆蟲及動物保育、綠建築...

我們的世界是一座驚喜連連的隱密花園。
